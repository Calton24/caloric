Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Caloric ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials (managed securely via Supabase Auth).

Health & Nutrition Data: Meal logs, calorie/macro entries, weight logs, and goal plans you enter are stored on your device and optionally synced to our servers.

Photos: When you use the camera feature to log meals, photos are processed by our AI service to identify food items. Photos are sent to our server for analysis and are not stored permanently on our servers.

Voice Input: If you use voice logging, audio is processed to extract meal descriptions and is not stored after processing.

Device Information: We may collect basic device information (OS version, app version) for crash reporting and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the calorie and nutrition tracking service
  • To analyze food photos using AI (OpenAI) for meal detection
  • To sync your data across devices when you are signed in
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs via anonymized crash reports

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and data storage
  • OpenAI: Food image analysis (photos are sent to OpenAI's API for processing)
  • Apple HealthKit: If you opt in, we read/write health data to Apple Health on your device
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and in-app purchase management

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. When synced, data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide the app's functionality.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Export all your data from the Settings screen
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Revoke Apple Health permissions at any time via iOS Settings

7. Children's Privacy

Caloric is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the app's support channels or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
